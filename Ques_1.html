Clear Cover Provision for RCC in Coastal Environment

Exposure Condition: Severe (Concrete exposed to coastal environment)

Recommended Clear Cover: 45 mm

Note: As per IS 456:2000 (Table 16), reinforced concrete elements subjected to severe exposure—such as coastal environments—require a minimum clear cover of 45 mm to ensure durability and protection against corrosion.
