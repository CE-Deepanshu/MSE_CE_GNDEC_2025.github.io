## Answer 1
---
The Clear cover be provided for a beam which is part of a bridge deck to be constructed over the Arabian Sea, along the western coast of Mumbai should be **50 mm**

**Note :** As per **IS 456:2000 (Table 16)**, reinforced concrete elements subjected to very severe environment, considering exposure condition - Concrete exposed to coastal environment, Concrete surfaces exposed to sea water spray. corrosive fumes or severe freezing 
conditions whilst wet such as coastal environments. Thats why it require a minimum clear cover of 50 mm to ensure durability and protection against corrosion.
