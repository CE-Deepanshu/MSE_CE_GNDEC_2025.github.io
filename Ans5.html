Answer 5.

Given Data

Effective span of beam, L = 4.75 + 0.3 = 5.05 m

Assume d = 500 mm, b = 300 mm

fck = 25 MPa, fy = 415 MPa

Mu = 239.1 kN·m = 239.1 × 10⁶ N·mm

Solution

Calculation of Steel Required (Mid-Span)

$$A_{st,req} = \frac{0.5 \times f_{ck}}{f_y} \left[ 1 - \sqrt{1 - \frac{4.6 \times M_u}{f_{ck} \times b \times d^2}} \right] \times b \times d$$ $$ = \frac{0.5 \times 25}{415} \left[ 1 - \sqrt{1 - \frac{4.6 \times 239.1 \times 10^6}{25 \times 300 \times 500^2}} \right] \times 300 \times 500$$

Ast,req = 1613.1 mm²

Provide 25mm diameter bars

$$Area of 1-25mm Ø bar = \frac{π}{4} \times 25^2 = 490.87 mm^2$$

$$No. of bars = \frac{A_{st,req}}{Area of 1 bar} = \frac{1613.1}{490.87} = 3.28 \approx 4 No.$$

Provide 4 nos of 25mm diameter bars

Actual Ast,provided = 4 × 490.87 = 1963.48 mm²

Steel Required at Support

$$A_{st,req(support)} = \frac{0.5 \times f_{ck}}{f_y} \left[ 1 - \sqrt{1 - \frac{4.6 \times M_5}{f_{ck} \times b \times d^2}} \right] \times b \times d$$ $$A_{st,req(support)} = \frac{0.5 \times 25}{415} \left[ 1 - \sqrt{1 - \frac{4.6 \times 351.25 \times 10^6}{25 \times 300 \times 500^2}} \right] \times 300 \times 500$$

Ast,req(support) = 1646.63 mm²

Provide 25mm diameter bars

$$Area of 1-25mm Ø bar = \frac{π}{4} \times 25^2 = 490.87 mm^2$$

$$No. of bars = \frac{A_{st,req}}{Area of 1 bar} = \frac{1646.63}{490.87} = 3.35 \approx 4 No.$$

No. of bars = Ast,req(support) / Area of 1 bar = 1646.63 / 490.87 = 3.35

Provide 4 nos of 25mm diameter bars

Actual Ast,provided = 4 × 490.87 = 1963.48 mm²
