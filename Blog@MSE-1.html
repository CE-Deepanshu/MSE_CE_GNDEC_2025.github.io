Introduction

Hello! I'm Deepanshu, a B.Tech Civil Engineering student at Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (CRN: 2214078). Under the guidance of my professor Dr. H. S. This blog documents my journey of transforming handwritten solutions into an accessible digital format.

The Challenge

The primary challenge was converting traditional handwritten engineering solutions into a clean, professional digital format. This involved:

  • Technical Documentation: Converting complex mathematical equations and structural diagrams
  • Formatting Consistency: Maintaining uniform presentation across all solutions
  • Digital Accessibility: Making content easily accessible and readable online
  • Code Implementation: Learning markdown coding for technical documentation

For the coding aspects, I referred to excellent resources like WebDev Simplified to understand web development fundamentals.

Implementation Steps

Phase 1: Content Digitization

  1. Scan and digitize handwritten solutions
  2. Use OCR tools for text conversion
  3. Verify accuracy of converted content

Phase 2: Technical Formatting

  1. Structure content using markdown
  2. Recreate diagrams in AutoCAD
  3. Implement proper mathematical notation

Phase 3: Quality Assurance

  1. Cross-verify all solutions
  2. Ensure consistent formatting
  3. Test accessibility and readability
